Tribute to Max Planck

Max Planck

What He Did

Max Plank was a German theoretical scientist. He laid the ground rules for modern physics, such as quantum physics and string theory. He thought up the size of quantum objects, which are the Plancks Length big or 1.6 x 10-35 m. He also developed Plancks Law, Constant, and many of the building blocks for quantum physics. Without Planck, we might not have the same modern physics that we have today.
